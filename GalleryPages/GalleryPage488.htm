Mother Earth and her incredible biodiversity are in serious trouble, suffering from the malignant proliferation and metastasis of our species. So now, to save ourselves and everything around us, the biological imperative to reproduce must be reversed. Can we do it? Can we use our incredible intellect to go against the instinctual grain and bring about population control? If so we will be the first species to ever voluntarily limit reproduction. We're also the only species with the forebrain and maybe even the heart to make it happen. In so doing we may save ourselves and our planet. We will affirm that we really are wise, foil Revelation, and demonstrate our mettle as the ultimate survivor. There is evidence that this is already taking place, at least in developed countries. But without the third world it will be too little, too late.
