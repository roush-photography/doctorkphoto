I found myself mystified by how it all worked, the chaos, the confusion, the mess. Animals galore and a diverse assemblage of people scurry hither and yon, in molcular-like motion, all seemingly with an important purpose. I never quite figured it out. One thing for sure, nothing in urban India is private, not even the most basic of bodily functions. Very soon you learn to go with the flow or get out. I stayed for a month!
